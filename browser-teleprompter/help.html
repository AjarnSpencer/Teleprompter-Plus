Teleprompter Plus Help

Core Features

Welcome to Teleprompter Plus! Here is a quick guide to its features:

  • Main Text Area: This is where you type or paste your script. Your text is saved automatically as you type.
  • Speed: Adjust the slider to control the scrolling speed.
  • Font Size: Adjust the slider to change the size of the text.
  • Background & Font Color: Use the color pickers to select your desired background and text colors for maximum readability.

Controls

  • Start/Stop: Toggles the automatic scrolling of the text.
  • Mirror: Flips the text horizontally (for use with physical teleprompter mirrors).
  • Reset: Instantly jumps the scroll position back to the top of your script.
  • Fullscreen: Toggles a distraction-free, fullscreen view.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Spacebar: Start or stop scrolling.
  • ArrowUp: Increase scrolling speed.
  • ArrowDown: Decrease scrolling speed.
  • ArrowRight: Jump forward in the script.
  • ArrowLeft: Jump backward in the script.
